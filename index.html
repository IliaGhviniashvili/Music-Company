Today, Our Music Company...
  • Operates 7 stores in Minnesota: 4 are in the Minneapolis/St. Paul metropolitan area and the others are in Duluth, Rochester and Virginia, MN.
  • Operates 5 stores in five other states: Fargo, ND; Sioux Falls, SD; Eau Claire, WI; Omaha, NE; and Denver, CO.
  • Employs about 200 people, many of whom are professional musicians.
  • Specializes in acoustic and digital pianos, band & orchestra Instruments, related musical accessories, printed music, repair and other services, and more!
  • Offers the best selection of woodwind, brass and orchestral string instruments in the Midwest! Visit us today at Schmitt Music Brooklyn Center to experience The Specialty Shops: The Flute Gallery, The Trombone Shop, The Trumpet Shop, The Saxophone Shop, and The Paul A. Schmitt Violin Shop!
  • Offers one of the largest and most complete selections of pianos in the nation.
  • Provides space in our stores for talented teachers to give over 185,000 music lessons each year.
  • Helps more than 13,000 students join their school band & orchestra programs each year.
  • Offers the services of approximately 40 technicians who repair and service thousands of instruments of all kinds every year.
